Service Description

We specialise in the supply, installation, testing, commissioning and maintenance of low-high voltage electric motors and emergency generating sets ranging from 0.55 kW to 5.0 MW and 500 VA to 5000 kVA respectively.
We also undertake motor rewinding and the repair of all types and sizes of welding machines.
